Toggle Autoplay

You may want the next video to play after the current video. You may wish to make sure this doesn't happen. If you're playing music, you want to keep the party going. If you're discussing an episode of a documentary for a science class, you want to keep the class focused.

To toggle autoplay on and off, press the autoplay button.
