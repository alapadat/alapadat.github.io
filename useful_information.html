Useful Information

This information is not necessary to operate YouTube as a beginner. Being familiar with YouTube terminology and its algorithm helps you navigate the platform. Video creators and regular users may reference this information. This section is meant to give you insights into YouTube culture and a high-level understanding of the website's code.

  • Watch History
    Watch History is every video you've ever watched. It is based on the tags, words, and popularity of videos you've watched, etc. YouTube takes what other people with similar Watch History have watched to suggest videos and creators, etc.
