Find Videos

Finding videos is easy with Youtube. But since Youtube is so large, finding exactly what you want can be tough.

Youtube has Home and Explore tabs. One option for finding new content is to scroll through them until you find a thumbnail/title that stand out.
Note: A thumbnail is the image that represents a video, like a movie's box art.

Once you are Subscribed to a few channels, you can find their videos in the Subscriptions tab.

If you watched a video recently but can't find it, the Library tab lets you view your watch History. If you've saved any playlists, they will be here. The Library tab keeps a playlist of every video you've ever liked.
