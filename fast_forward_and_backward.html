Fast Forward and Rewind

If you're looking for information/missed something, you can fast forward or rewind the video to find it.

When you fast-forward/rewind, your place in the video moves 5 seconds forward/backward. If you want to move more, press the appropriate key repeatedly.
  1. To rewind, press the left arrow keyon your keyboard.
  2. To fast forward, press the right arrow keyon your keyboard.
