Subscriptions Tab

The subscriptions tab keeps track of every channel you're subscribed too, as well as their videos.

The subscriptions Tab has 3 parts.
  • Grid displays all videos in chronological order, from left to right.
  • List displays all videos in chronoligical order, stacked from top to bottom. Channel names are displayed prominently.
  • Manage displays all subscriptions in alphabetical order, stacked from top to bottom.
