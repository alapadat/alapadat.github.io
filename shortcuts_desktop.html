Desktop Shortcuts

If you use YouTube a lot, shortcuts save a lot of time.

You may not think pressing K on your keyboard is a lot faster than pressing the Play/Pause toggle.

Pressing the K key to pause takes less than a second.

To use a mouse to toggle Play/Pause, you have to take your hand off your keyboard, put your hand on your mouse/trackpad, move your cursor to the Play/Pause button, and press it.

The seconds you spend with your hand off your keyboard could be used for something else.

Even if you're just watching YouTube, looking for the proper toggle or control breaks your focus.
