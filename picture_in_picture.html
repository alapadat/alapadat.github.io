Picture in Picture

With Picture in Picture, you can keep watching a YouTube video without being in the tab that's playing the video.

Picture in Picture is useful for tutorials and multitasking. While in Picture in Picture mode, you can:
  • Pause/Play
  • Fast Forward/Rewind
  • Return to Mini Player
Any settings you've adjusted will be translated into Picture in Picture mode. To adjust them again, you must return to playing the video in its tab.
Note: You can not use shortcuts in Picture in Picture mode.
